Services

Commercial pool and spa maintenance services.

PPC LLC keeps commercial aquatic facilities cleaner, safer, and easier to manage through scheduled service, equipment awareness, and responsive communication.

Commercial Focus Routine care, technical awareness, and practical reporting for high-use facilities.
01

Commercial Pool Maintenance

Routine pool maintenance for high-use commercial properties, with attention to water chemistry, filtration, surface condition, debris removal, and overall presentation.

  • Water chemistry testing and balancing
  • Sanitizer and chemical feeder checks
  • Skimming, brushing, vacuuming, and tile-line care
  • Filter cleaning, backwashing, and pressure checks
  • Visual equipment inspections
  • Preventative maintenance recommendations
02

Commercial Spa Maintenance

Commercial spa service for properties where heat, bather load, and sanitizer demand require disciplined routines.

  • Sanitization and water balance
  • Circulation and jet checks
  • Filter maintenance
  • Cleaning and presentation support
03

Equipment Repair & Troubleshooting

Repair and diagnostic support for core pool and spa systems when operation, safety, water clarity, or reliability is affected.

  • Pumps, motors, and circulation systems
  • Filters, valves, and plumbing components
  • Heaters and automation checks
  • Chemical feeders and controllers
04

Acid Washing & Surface Restoration

Surface cleaning and restoration support when staining, scale, or buildup affects appearance and normal cleaning is no longer enough.

05

Emergency Service

Responsive service for urgent commercial pool or spa issues affecting equipment, water quality, circulation, access, safety, or normal operation.

06

Emergency Bio Cleanup

When a biological contamination incident closes a commercial pool or spa, response time matters. PPC LLC provides emergency bio cleanup service focused on completing the necessary treatment and recovery process efficiently, restoring safe operating conditions, and helping the property return the aquatic facility to service as soon as possible.

  • Rapid response to biological contamination incidents
  • Treatment and recovery procedures
  • Water chemistry recovery and verification
  • Filtration and circulation support during recovery
  • Operational checks before returning the facility to service
  • Focus on reducing unnecessary downtime while restoring safe conditions
07

Health Department Compliance Support

Support for Southern Nevada Health District inspection readiness through consistent maintenance routines, water chemistry attention, equipment checks, and clear issue reporting.

PPC LLC supports compliance readiness; property owners and operators remain responsible for their regulatory obligations.
